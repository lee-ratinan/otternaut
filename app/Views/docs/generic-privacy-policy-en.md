OtterNova (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides online business tools and booking systems designed to make scheduling and operations simpler.
We care about privacy and collect only what is necessary to deliver our services, nothing more.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

 - OtterNova Main Website (marketing site)
 - OtterNova Marketplace Website (for end-customers)
 - OtterNova Backend System (for business users)

## 1. Information We Collect
### 1.1 Main Website (Marketing Site)

We do not collect personal information.
We only use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and improve our content.

This includes:

 - Pages visited
 - Browser/OS/device information
 - Approximate location (city-level)
 - Time spent on site

**No** names, emails, phone numbers, or sensitive data are collected here.

### 1.2 Marketplace Website (End-Customer Use)

This site allows end-users to book services or buy products from businesses using OtterNova.

We collect only the minimum data required for booking or order fulfillment, such as:

For service bookings:

 - Name
 - Email address
 - (Optional) Phone number

For product purchases:

 - Name
 - Email address
 - Shipping address (only if delivery is required)
 - (Optional) Phone number

No address is collected if:

 - The product is digital, or
 - The customer chooses self pick-up

We do **not** collect unnecessary or sensitive information.

### 1.3 Backend System (For Businesses Using OtterNova)

Business users use our platform to manage operations.
We collect and store information required for system functionality, including:

 - Business profile
 - Branches and branch addresses
 - Services and service details
 - Products, prices, stock, and related configurations
 - Staff information (names, roles, emails)
 - Customer booking and order data

We do **not** use this operational data for advertising, tracking, or selling to third parties.

## 2. How We Use the Information

We use collected data for purposes such as:

 - Providing booking and scheduling features
 - Processing orders and payments
 - Communicating booking confirmations or updates
 - Managing business accounts and system functions
 - Improving platform performance and reliability
 - Securing accounts and preventing misuse

We do **not** sell, rent, trade, or share personal data with external marketers or unrelated third parties.

## 3. Data Security

We store data using industry-level security standards similar to what is used in banking and fintech systems.
This includes:

 - Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
 - Secured databases
 - Access controls
 - Regular security checks

Only authorized personnel can access data when necessary to maintain the system.

## 4. Data Retention and Anonymization

We retain user information only as long as required for operational or legal purposes.

If a user or business requests deletion:

 - The data will be deleted or anonymized
 - Anonymization will be used if deleting the record would break system integrity (e.g., business analytics, transaction history)

We do not keep personal data longer than needed.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

 - Access your data
 - Correct inaccurate information
 - Request data deletion
 - Export or transfer your data
 - Withdraw consent for optional processing

We will respond to these requests within a reasonable time.

Businesses using OtterNova can export their operational data at any time and may stop using our service whenever they choose.
